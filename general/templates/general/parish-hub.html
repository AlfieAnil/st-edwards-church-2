What is the Parish Hub

Our Parish Hub is our single point of contact, which, together with our website, is able to answer all general enquiries or requests. If they cannot provide an answer at the time they will pass your message to whoever has responsibility for the relevant activity in the parish. We will always try to get back to you by the next working day. The Hub is operated 6 days a week for 4 hours each day by our Administration Team under the leadership of the Parish Administrator. The Hub can be contacted via the Contact Form on this website, by email to : stedward@rcaol.org.uk or by telephone on: 01942 203583
